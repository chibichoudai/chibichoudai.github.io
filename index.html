ABOUT

Introduction
Hello! We are a team of artists, designers, and engineers eager to share our love of anime and video games with the community. Based in the Northeast, we are always looking to explore new ideas and meet new fellow enthusiasts at Cons and meetups!



Mission
We specialize in chibi (tiny, small, cute) versions of anime, manga, and video game characters! Through our designs, we seek to capture the spirit and style of the original character in a cute, miniaturized package to make a truly special product.
